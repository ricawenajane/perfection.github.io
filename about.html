Our Company

Welcome to Petal Perfection, a charming flower boutique nestled in the heart of Baguio City. Founded just a year ago by a trio of passionate friends and florists, Petal Perfection is a testament to our shared love for blooms and beauty. Despite balancing our roles as business owners and students, we pour our hearts into curating exquisite floral arrangements that reflect the natural splendor of our beloved hometown. With a dedication to quality craftsmanship and personalized service, we invite you to explore our floral haven and let us add a touch of perfection to your special moments..
